Privacy Policy for SCMP

Effective Date: 2023-11-13

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information through the App. The App only accesses and plays music files that are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to, store, or share any personal data related to your music files.

Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES: To access and display images associated with media files (not used for image data collection).
  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO: To access and play audio (music) files stored on your device.
  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO: To access and display video files associated with media files (not used for video data collection).
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To read music files stored on your device.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To write necessary data for the app's functionality (e.g., playlists).

Log Data

We do not collect any log data from the App.

Cookies

We do not use cookies in the App.

Service Providers

We do not employ any third-party companies or individuals to facilitate our App or provide services on our behalf.
